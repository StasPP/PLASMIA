I. ENGLISH:

1. Colors:
  0 - empty
  1 - Red
  2 - Orange
  3 - Yellow
  4 - Green
  5 - Sky blue
  6 - Dark blue
  7 - Violet
  8 - White (for closed doors)


2. Names and numbers of items and bonuses

  Items
  1 - repair1 - repair set (small)
  2 - telescope - telescope (normal)
  3 - hispeed - accelerator
  4 - mina - mines set
  5 - rock - rockets set
  6 - repair2 - big repair set
  7 - rainbow - rainbow plasma
  8 - antirock - antirocket shield
  9 - boom - superbomb

  Bonuses: 
  10 - radar - Radar(MiniMap)
  11 - dopslots - add 2 color slots 
  12 - lakmus - color detector
  13 - plasmup - 2x plasma damage
  14 - droid - repair droid
  15 - inf - infinity plasm

  Additional items (w/o numbers):
  superrock - big (100) rocket set (secret)
  Superscope - long-acting telescope (secret)
  hispeed2 - long-acting accelerator (secret)
  
  Additional bonuses (w/o numbers):
  detect - color lamps sonar
